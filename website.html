Our Journey

The QuREE project was born from a simple observation: To bridge the gap between physical and remote presence, enabling immersive interaction through VR and autonomous mobility. It combines smart sensing, environment awareness, and telepresence to perform tasks in real-world environments safely and efficiently.

February 2025

Project Inception

Initial concept development and feasibility studies during our lab briefings.

March - April 2025

Prototype Development

Development of hardware and functioning code.

April - May 2025

Technology Integration

PCB design and soldering, integration of software and hardware. Assembly of the project.

May 2025

Testing & Debugging

Extensive testing to refine the interface and interaction model. Fixing of various bugs faced in line folloowing and obstacle avoidance.

May 2025

Final Product

QuREE reaches its current form, ready for showcase at the Engineering Expo.
